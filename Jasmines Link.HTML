Research Overview
Hi! My name is Jasmine! This summer I had the privilege to work in the Shalek Lab under Dr. Alex Shalek with mentors Sarah Quinn and Ankit Basak. I really enjoyed getting to know everyone, working on collaborative projects, and learning all about different infectious diseases. I got to learn different wet lab methodologies and techniques of Single-Cell RNA Sequencing. Further, I also worked on computational data analysis in R Studio. RISE has allowed me to explore the different aspects of research in medicine and in the future, I hope to pursue a career in Oncology and apply research in a clinical setting.

Throughout the course of the summer, I worked on a dataset that was part of a larger project that my mentor was working on which consisted of analyzing the characteristics of two different cell lines. The ultimate goal was to make comparisons between the Human Embryonic Kidney cell line and the Pancreatic Cancer cell line to better understand the baseline gene expression of the lines for future perturbation experiments.
